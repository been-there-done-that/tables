A — New tab in bottom panel

Explain gets its own tab alongside Results and Logs. Inside it has sub-tabs: Plan Tree, Table, Stats, AI. Familiar pattern — same space as results.

B — Side-by-side split

Results on left, Explain plan on right. Both visible at once when explain is active. No sub-tabs — explain shows inline tree. Maximises simultaneous info but tight on space.

C — Full panel, explain replaces results when active

Clicking "Explain" switches the bottom panel to a full explain view with Tree/Table/Stats sub-views and a prominent "Ask AI" button. Click Results tab to go back to data.
